Why EdTech is hard

Education is a high-trust, high-stakes decision

A course or school isn't an impulse buy — families research for weeks and need proof before they commit. Generic marketing doesn't cut it.

Trust is everything

Parents & students compare heavily — credibility and outcomes decide it.

Seasonal demand

Admission cycles spike — you need to be ready and visible at the right moment.

Rising acquisition cost

Ad costs keep climbing — you need efficient, intent-led channels.

Research-heavy buyers

They Google & ask AI everything — you must show up across every surface.

EdTech FAQ

Your questions, answered

We market EdTech brands in four moves: build trust (branding, faculty & success proof), get found (SEO, AEO and GEO for the questions families ask Google and AI), fill seats (performance-marketing lead-gen and demo campaigns), and convert better (UX and funnels that turn demos into enrolments). The exact mix depends on whether you're a course platform, coaching institute, school or edu-SaaS.
Paid lead-gen can bring enquiries and demo bookings within days, with conversion improving over the first 30–60 days as we test. SEO, AEO and GEO compound over 3–6 months and become your most cost-efficient enrolment channel. We plan launches around your admission seasons.
A blend: local SEO and Google for high-intent searches ("best NEET coaching near me"), Meta & Google performance ads for demo bookings, and social proof on Instagram/YouTube (results, toppers, reels). Seasonal scaling around admission windows matters most.
